Maximum Decks = 10
Player Actions = setup, hit, stay

RULES
1. All players are dealt 2 initial cards from the deck
2. Dealer asks each player to "hit" or "stay"
3. Once each player either stays OR busts (goes over 21), the Dealer hits until hand is at least 17
4. The hand that is closest to 21 and didn't bust WINS
